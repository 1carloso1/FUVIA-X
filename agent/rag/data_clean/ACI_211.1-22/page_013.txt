5.2—Selection process The selection process begins by estimating the required batch weights for the concrete. The estimation involves a sequence of logical, straightforward steps that, in effect, fit the characteristics of the available materials into a mixture suitable for the work. The job specifications may dictate
some or all of the following: (a) Maximum w/cm (b) Minimum cementitious materials content (c) Air content (d) Slump (ec) Nominal maximum size of aggregate (f) Strength (g) Other requirements relating to required average strength, admixtures, and special types of cement, other cementitious materials, or aggregate
