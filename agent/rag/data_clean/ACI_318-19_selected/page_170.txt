[CODE] 26.5.4 Concreting in cold weather

[COMMENTARY] R26.5.4 Concreting in cold weather
[COMMENTARY] Detailed recommendations for cold weather concreting are given in ACI 306R. Specification requirements for concreting in cold weather are provided in ACI 301 and
ACI 306.1. If both ACI 301 and ACI 306.1 are referenced in construction documents, the governing requirements should
be identified.

[CODE] 26.5.4.1 Design information:
[CODE] (a) Temperature limits for concrete as delivered in cold
weather.

[COMMENTARY] R26.5.4.1(a) ASTM C94, ACI 306R, and ACI 301 contain requirements and recommendations for concrete tempera- ture based on section size.

[CODE] 26.5.4.2 Compliance requirements:
[CODE] (a) Adequate equipment shall be provided for heating concrete materials and protecting concrete during freezing or near-freezing weather. (b) Frozen materials or materials containing ice shall not be used. (c) Forms, fillers, and ground with which concrete is to
come in contact shall be free from frost and ice.
