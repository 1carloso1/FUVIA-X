[CODE] 26.4.1.6 Steel fiber reinforcement

[COMMENTARY] R26.4.1.6 Steel fiber reinforcement

[CODE] 26.4.1.6.1 Compliance requirements:
[CODE] (a) Steel fiber remnforcement used for shear resistance shall satisfy (1) and (2): (1) Be deformed and conform to ASTM A820. (2) Have a length-to-diameter ratio of at least 50 and not
exceeding 100.

[COMMENTARY] R26.4.1.6.1(a) Deformations in steel fibers enhance mechanical anchorage with the concrete. The limits for the fiber length-to-diameter ratio are based on available test data (Parra-Montesinos 2006). Because data are not avail- able on the potential for corrosion problems due to galvanic action, the use of deformed steel fibers in members rein- forced with stainless-steel bars or galvanized steel bars is not recommended.
