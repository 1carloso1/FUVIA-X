of special structural walls including coupling beams and wall piers forming part of the seismic-force-resisting system.

[COMMENTARY] This section contains requirements for the dimensions
and details of special structural walls and all components including coupling beams and wall piers. Wall piers are defined in Chapter 2. Design provisions for vertical wall
