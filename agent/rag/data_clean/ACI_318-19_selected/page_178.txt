[COMMENTARY] R26.6.4.2(b) “Tack” welding (welding crossing bars) can seriously weaken a bar at the point welded by creating a metallurgical notch effect. This operation can be performed safely only when the material welded and welding operations are under continuous competent control, as in the manufacture of welded wire reinforcement. Welding of anchor bars at the front face of brackets or corbels is addressed in R16.5.6.3.
