[COMMENTARY] R9.7.6.3 Torsion

[CODE] 9.7.6.3.1 If required, transverse torsional reinforcement shall be closed stirrups satisfying 25.7.1.6 or hoops.

[COMMENTARY] R9.7.6.3.1 The stirrups are required to be closed because inclined cracking due to torsion may occur on all faces of a member. In the case of sections subjected primarily to torsion, the concrete side cover over the stirrups spalls off at high torsional moments (Mitchell and Collins 1976).
