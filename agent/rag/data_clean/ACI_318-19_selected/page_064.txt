[CODE] 10.7.4 Offset bent longitudinal reinforcement
[CODE] 10.7.4.1 The slope of the inclined portion of an offset
bent longitudinal bar relative to the longitudinal axis of the
column shall not exceed 1 in 6. Portions of bar above and
below an offset shall be parallel to axis of column.
[CODE] 10.7.4.2 If the column face is offset 3 in. or more, longi- tudinal bars shall not be offset bent and separate dowels, lap spliced with the longitudinal bars adjacent to the offset
column faces, shall be provided.

[COMMENTARY] R10.7.5.1 General

[CODE] 10.7.5.1 General
[CODE] 10.7.5.1.1 Lap splices, mechanical splices, butt-welded splices, and end-bearing splices shall be permitted.

[COMMENTARY] R10.7.5.1.2 Frequently, the basic gravity load combina- tion will govern the design of the column itself, but a load
combination including wind or earthquake effects may induce greater tension in some column bars. Each bar splice should
be designed for the maximum calculated bar tensile force.

[CODE] 10.7.5.1.2 Splices shall satisfy requirements for all factored load combinations.
