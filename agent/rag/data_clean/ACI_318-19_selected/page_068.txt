10.7.6.4 Lateral support of offset bent longitudinal bars
10.7.6.4.1 Where longitudinal bars are offset, horizontal
support shall be provided by ties, hoops, spirals, or parts
of the floor construction and shall be designed to resist 1.5
times the horizontal component of the calculated force in the inclined portion of the offset bar.
10.7.6.4.2 If transverse reinforcement is provided to resist
forces that result from offset bends, ties, hoops, or spirals shall be placed not more than 6 in. from points of bend.
