[CODE] 18.14.6.1 Wall piers not designated as part of the seismic- force-resisting system shall satisfy the requirements of 18.10.8. Where the general building code includes provi- sions to account for overstrength of the seismic-force- resisting system, it shall be permitted to calculate the design shear force as Q, times the shear induced under design displacements, 6,,.

[COMMENTARY] R18.14.6.1 Section 18.10.8 requires that the design shear force be determined according to 18.7.6.1, which in some cases may result in unrealistically large forces. As an alterna- tive, the design shear force can be determined as the product
of an overstrength factor and the shear induced when the wall pier is displaced by 6,. The overstrength factor Q, included in FEMA P749, ASCE/SEI 7, and the 2018 IBC can be used for this purpose.
