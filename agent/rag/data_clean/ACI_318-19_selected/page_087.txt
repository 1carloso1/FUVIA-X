[CODE] 18.6.5 Shear strength

[COMMENTARY] R18.6.5 Shear strength
[COMMENTARY] Unless a beam possesses a moment strength that is on the order of 3 or 4 times the design moment, it should be
assumed that it will yield in flexure in the event of a major earthquake. The design shear force should be selected so as to be a good approximation of the maximum shear that may develop in a member. Therefore, required shear strength for frame members is related to flexural strengths of the designed member rather than to factored shear forces indi- cated by lateral load analysis.
